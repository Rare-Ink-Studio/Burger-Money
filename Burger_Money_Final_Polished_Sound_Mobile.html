Meal Quest

Collect burger money, climb longer scenes, dodge DROP obstacles, and earn enough for the ultimate meal prize. This is a fast-food-inspired arcade game with saved local scoreboards.

🪙 Collect money and keep combos going.
☁️ Jump cloud-to-cloud through longer scenes.
🚀 Grab power-ups: rocket, magnet, shield, mega jump.
🚫 Hit DROP and you restart at the bottom of that scene.

Keyboard: arrows/A-D to move, up/space/W to jump. Mobile: use the buttons.

How to Play

Move left and right to land on clouds. Tap JUMP for a double jump. Each scene is longer now, and the game only moves you forward when you complete both the climb and money goal.

🛡 Shield blocks one DROP hit.
🧲 Magnet pulls money toward you.
🚀 Rocket gives a short climb boost.
💥 Mega Jump launches you higher.
